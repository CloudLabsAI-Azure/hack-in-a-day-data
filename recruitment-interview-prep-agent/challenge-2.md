# Challenge 02: Upload Job Description and Resume Knowledge Documents

## Introduction
To help the HR Interview Copilot evaluate candidates and generate role-specific interview questions, we need to enrich it with relevant hiring reference documents.  
These documents will later support resume-to-job matching, scoring, and interview preparation by providing grounding information such as required skills, responsibilities, and experience expectations.

In this challenge, you will upload **Job Description PDFs and Resume PDFs** to the Copilot knowledge base.

## Accessing the Datasets

Please download and extract the datasets required for this challenge here:

```
https://github.com/CloudLabsAI-Azure/hack-in-a-day-challenges/archive/refs/heads/hr-datasets.zip
```

## ⚠️ Important Data Notice

- Do NOT upload real candidate resumes.
- Use only lab-provided documents.
- Resume data is sensitive HR information.

## Challenge Objectives
- Add job descriptions and resumes as knowledge sources for the Copilot.
- Ensure all documents are fully processed.
- Enable the Copilot to reference these documents during responses.

## Steps to Complete
1. From the Copilot Studio home screen, open the **HR Interview Copilot** created previously.

1. In top navigation bar, select **Knowledge**.

1. Click **+ Add Knowledge**.

1. Choose **Upload files**.

1. Upload the **Job Description** and **Resume** documents provided, which you downloaded and extracted earlier.

1. After uploading, verify each file shows the status **Ready**.

   > Note: It may take up to 30 minutes for files to finish processing. You may continue to the next challenge while processing completes.

## Success Criteria
- All Job Description and Resume documents are uploaded and processed without errors.
- The Copilot can now reference information from the uploaded hiring documents to evaluate candidates and generate interview questions.
